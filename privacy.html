NUZU News Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

NUZU News is a free news aggregation service that links to articles from third-party publishers. We do not host, reproduce, or store any article content.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. NUZU News does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind.

Local Storage

NUZU uses your browser's localStorage to save your personal preferences — such as saved articles and dark/light mode settings — directly on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third Party Links

All articles link to external publishers. Once you leave NUZU News, you are subject to the privacy policies of those publishers.

Children

NUZU News is a general news service not directed at children under 13.
